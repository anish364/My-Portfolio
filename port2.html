Hi, I'm Anish Gurung

A BCA Student

Hey there! I'm a BCA student with a wild imagination, a stubborn keyboard, and a dream — to build games that feel like stories and play like magic. I'm currently diving deep into the world of game development, blending my love for coding with my obsession for great gameplay and immersive design. From late-night UE experiments to doodling characters in the margins of my notes, I'm all in — learning, building, breaking stuff, and learning again. When I'm not debugging or watching game dev tutorials at 2 AM, I'm sketching, jamming on my guitar, or getting emotionally attached to fictional characters (don't judge). My goal? To create games that spark joy, trigger feels, or just make someone say, “Whoa… that was cool.” This portfolio is a snapshot of my journey — raw, real, and growing. Feel free to explore. Feedback is gold. Glitches are temporary. Creativity is forever. Let's make some pixels dance.
